The Rubik's Cube: a deceptively simple and beautifully complex puzzle..

Growing up, I always had a Rubik's Cube lying around the house. I found it to be a marvelous puzzle, simple and complex. Even my dad couldn't figure out it's mysteries! It wasn't until I married Hazel that I started to uncover the secrets of the cube. I learned the algorithms to solve them; learning the algorithms is a deceptively simple process, but the math behind the solutions are complex.
