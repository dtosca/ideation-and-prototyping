Considering the theme of my time capsule, I decided to pay homage to a staple of every Cuban household - a dominoes set.

I created my prototype out of cardboard to see the concept. Ideally, I'd make the final time capsule out of wood, like a real dominoes set.

I made a few sketches and looked at some inspiration photos, and finally settled on a dominoes box with a sliding lid, similar to one my family had.

Working with cardboard was a little tricky, particularly when it came to the adhesive material. I looked at this guide for making a cardboard box with a sliding lid.

Finally, I decorated the box with colorful artwork to make it look like a real dominoes set.

Next steps

For the final time capsule, I'm planning on transfering this design to wood. I will probably make the dimensions smaller, it's a bit tall now. Then, I'll laser cutting the designs onto the panels, and paint them.
